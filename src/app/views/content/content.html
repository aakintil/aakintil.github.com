claron

providing clarity in crisis

Active Shooter situations in America have been on the rise in recent years. Working with Exitus Technologies, we helped actualise Claron. A platform for seamless communication, information alignment, and status updates in emergency situations. Allowing paramedics, police, and family members access to a shared pool of information. With new documentation and research findings, Exitus improved their on-boarding process and built the first version of Claron.
